## The Mission in One Sentence

Build a living architecture of beautiful information that stays legible as it grows — for humans and for AI.

---

## What "Empire" Means Here

Not scale for its own sake. A Notion empire means:

- **A living catalog of everything I've done** — work, projects, thinking, creative output — rich enough that future AI collaborators can understand how I think and help me move faster
- **Complexity that grows with me**, with Notion, and with AI — without growing chaos
- **A system I actually live in**, not one I maintain for appearances. Built in public where useful; maintained privately where necessary
- **Anti-decay as a design requirement** — existing structures are pruned or maintained before they rot. A beautiful system that decays isn't beautiful; it's abandoned

The identity anchor for how it should feel: *"Sometimes it was beautiful watching him work."* Elegance. Legibility. Flow.

---

## Why Notion (Not Something Else)

- It's the first tool that genuinely fits a worldbuilding brain: thousands of pages, structure + freedom, relational thinking, stats and tallies
- I'm going to live in it regardless — building with intention makes it maintained and accountable
- The combination of AI-operability (agents, MCP, Autofill) and human legibility is uniquely matched to how I work: technology as leverage for writing and building, not a substitute for either

---

## Audience + Privacy Model

**Primary readers:** Me + my AIs + my agents. This system is private-first.

Three tiers of visibility govern everything built here:

| Tier | What it is | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| **Core System** | The full workspace — private, complete, truthful | Never assumes an audience. Never optimized for watchability over accuracy. |
| **Selective Surfaces** | Sanitized snapshots, templates, demos derived from the core | Built intentionally for sharing. Never the live infrastructure. |
| **Broadcast Content** | Streams, posts, public-facing communication | Communication layer only. Not infrastructure. Not a driver of system decisions. |

**The protection this provides:** The system never becomes performative. "Public proof" is intentional and derived — it doesn't shape the core.
